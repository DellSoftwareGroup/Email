RemoteScan can help you solve the problem of connecting imaging devices such as scanners and cameras in virtual environments. It works with any TWAIN- or WIA-compliant scanner or camera, and with any TWAIN-compliant scanning application. Additionally, RemoteScan supports all virtual environments - vWorkspace, Ctirix, VMware, or Windows Terminal Server.
In addition to enabling scanner redirection, RemoteScan allows you to:
  • Set defaults and templates to take the guesswork out of scanner settings
  • Share scanners and cameras with othe rusers on the network
  • Ensure a secure document scanning workflow, as no scanned images are stored on the local workstation
